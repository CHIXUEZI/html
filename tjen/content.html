Introduction to the School of Civil Engineering and Architecture

May 18,2018    Editor： admin

 


Henan University has always attached great importance to the development of the School of Civil Engineering and Architecture. In May 1931, the then-president Mr. Xinwu Xu, a specialist in Civil Engineering and Water Conservancy, proposed to set up the Department of Civil Engineering and appointed the first dean of the department, Wenlin Qu (styled as Fuzhang). Later the discipline was merged into another institution because of national adjustments. In 1946, Kai Yan, a well-known water conservancy expert, served as the dean of the Department of Civil Engineering at the then National Henan University. The reconstruction of the department was conducted; however, it was soon closed again due to war and other reasons. In 1988, the school established the Department of Arts and Crafts and Architectural Engineering in accordance with the needs of national economic construction. In January 1994, the Department of Construction Engineering was divided from the Department of Arts and Crafts. Finally, the School of Civil Engineering and Architecture was established in April 2002. It currently has Department of Architectural Engineering, Department of Road and Bridge Engineering, Department of Architecture, Department of Urban and Rural Planning, Institute of Materials and Structure, Institute of Steel and Space Structure, Research Institute of Ancient Landscape Design, Research Institute of Architectural Design and other teaching and research institutions.

The school currently has 91 faculty members and 72 professional teachers, including 1 part-time academician, 2 Yellow River scholars, 2 distinguished professors, 17 professors, and 25 associate professors. Among them, there is 1 senior engineer, 1 senior experimenter, 1 famous teacher in Henan Province, 4 eminent teachers in Henan Province and 4 academic and technical leaders in the Education Department of Henan Province. Thirty five of the staff have doctoral degrees while 5 of them are national registered architects, 12 national registered structure engineers, 1 national registered public equipment engineers ( HVAC), and 4 national registered civil engineers(geotechnical). We have fostered a team with high academic levels, professional abilities, and an innovative spirit. Our team members are mostly young and middle-aged teachers with high academic qualifications. The school offers 3 undergraduate programs in Civil Engineering, Architecture and Urban and Rural Planning; 3 first-level academic graduate programs in Civil Engineering, Materials Science and Engineering (materials science), and Design Science (architectural design and theory, architectural history and theoretical direction); and 2 professional graduate programs in Engineering (architectural and ivil Engineering) and Education (Professional Education - Civil Engineering and Hydraulics). The two first-level disciplines, Civil Engineering and Architecture, are also key disciplines at the provicial level, with the former accredited as the Outstanding Engineer Project of Henan Province (the pilot project of comprehensive reform of engineering education in Henan Province). A number of academic clusters such as Civil Engineering and Architecture History and Theory have formed.

In accordance with the guiding ideology of running a school with a thick foundation, wide calibre, and strong ability, the school adopts a student-centered approach to cultivate innovative and all-round talents. It attaches great importance to the construction of a distinctive style of study and implements quality education in all respects. In order to establish a platform for undergraduate education, the school has built an Laboratory of Architectural Technology, a Laboratory of Recycled and Functional Building Materials, a Laboratory of Architectural Technology, a Foundation Laboratory of Civil Engineering and Architecture, a Laboratory of Structural Engineering, a CAD Laboratory of Civil engineering and Architectural, and a Geotechnical Engineering Laboratory. The Civil Engineering Experimental Teaching Center is an experimental teaching demonstration center of Henan Province, and the Virtual Simulation Experimental Teaching Center of Civil Engineering is accredited as the virtual simulation experimental teaching center of Henan Province. The laboratories scatter around Building A, B and D of the 16th Group of the Jinming Campus. With a total construction area of around 10,000 square meters, our labs have nearly 2050 sets (sets) of equipment which are worth at 22.82 million yuan in total.

Our school sets academic exchange as a high priority. We have already established academic contacts with universities and colleges in the United States, Japan, Taiwan, Hong Kong, and other countries and regions. We also have business contacts with more than 20 colleges and universities in China. In addition, we have signed industry-learning-research cooperation agreements with 17 enterprises including Zhejiang Bada Constructioin Group Co., Ltd which ranks among the top 500 Chinese enterprises, Henan Provincial Architectural Design Institute and Henan Sanjian Construction Engineering Group. We have signed employment base agreements with 10 companies. Moreover, Henan Province Urban and Rural Planning Institute set up a "Provincial Planning Institute" scholarship for our students. We have also launched a joint education project with the University of Miami. In order to improve the quality of education and scientific research, we have invited academicians Jun Sun, Shilin Dong and Jingtang He, and other well-known domestic and foreign experts and scholars to make academic reports on the new progress of the disciplines.

The school attaches great importance to the quality of personnel training. Through the concerted efforts of party and government leaders and teachers and students, we have made breakthroughs and achieved outstanding results. At present, we have enrolled around 1,200 undergraduates, 1300 students of related majors from domestic and foreign cooperative projects and more than 1800 adult education students. There are about 2,600 students at school. Our students have won numerous awards in the National Architectural Design and Architectural Modeling Competition, the National Concrete Material Design Contest, the National Undergraduate Mathematics Competition, and the Structural Design Competition for Civil Engineering in the six Central South Provinces, setting a record for the colleges and universities in Henan Province. The employment rate of graduates has always been among the highest, reaching over 95%, while the entrance examination rate for graduate students is about 30%. Over the past 30 years, the school has trained more than 5,000 undergraduate graduates for the country, covering more than a dozen provinces and regions including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangdong, Zhejiang, Henan, etc. Our graduates have received warm welcome from the society because of their virtues, talent and working capabilities. Most of them have become the backbone of their working units. Many graduates have become national registered engineers, architects, constructors, and supervision engineers, undertaking great responsibilities in their working place.

The school focuses on strengthening the discipline construction, increasing investment on scientific research, and effectively enhancing its competitiveness. In recent years, our faculty has published more than 40 books; undertaken more than 40 scientific projects by National Social Science Foundation, National Natural Science Foundation, and Henan Province and over 22 education reform projects at school level or above; compiled 1 national-level planned textbook; received 3 teaching achievement awards at provincial level; opened 1 top-quality resource sharing course at provincial level; and published and edited 22 monographs and textbooks. Among the 800 academic papers published by our faculty, more than 300 papers were indexed in SCI, EI, and ISTP. The Nanyang International Yucheng Project designed by our institute adopts the innovative structural system of steel frame and folding plate lattice shell, which won us the second prize of the Outstanding Engineering Survey and Design Industry Award of Henan Province in 2011. The Design Institute and the Ancient Building Institute undertook the planning and design projects concerning Kang Manor in Gong Yi and Dahong Century City in Kaifeng, making due contributions to local economic construction and social development.

At present, the School of Civil Engineering and Architecture has formulated its “13th Five-Year” development plan. The staff is working hard to promote great development of the school with great enthusiasm and work attitudes featuring unity, diligence, practical work and innovation.

The emblem of the school: The emblem of the School of Civil Engineering and Architecture was designed by Ke Feng, a faculty member.

Design description of the emblem :

11. The logo is mainly composed of two characters: soil and wood. The overall shape resembles the word "construction", which is similar to the three-dimensional shape of a building.


2The shape of the logo is hexagonal. Take the meaning of six-space, which means that civil construction is the design of the architectural space. From a distance, it resembles a large sailing ship, implying that the School of Civil Engineering and Architecture will raise our sail to brave winds and waves .

3The two colors of the logo, yellow and blue, represent the combination of yin and yang. There is no end to end, and there is no end to it.
